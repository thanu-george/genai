    ATTENTION

    All the services provided by Invest India (National Investment Promotion and Facilitation Agency of India) to the investors are free of cost.

    Please note that Invest India does not solicit/accept any amount or security deposit from investors for providing its services or from job seekers during the selection process or while inviting candidates for an interview. It has been observed that Ponzi schemes by unauthorized individuals using names like Invest India, Invest India Global Entertainer, and other fictitious entities are in circulation. They are attempting to deceive people into investing and soliciting job applications and requiring the job seekers / applicants to pay processing fees or deposit amount by sending false e-mails, messages or by making fraudulent telephone calls. If any person receives any unsolicited or fraudulent communication offering an investment opportunity or a job offer or interview call from Invest India against payment of money, it is suggested not to respond. On receipt of such communication, the investor or candidate may take measures such as visiting the official website of Invest India to get the contact details to enquire about the genuinely of such information. For further information on Invest India, please visit:

    www.investindia.gov.in/  www.indiainvestmentgrid.gov.in

    You are urged to be cautious while opening links or attachments from unknown third parties. Any communication suggesting any payment is contrary to Invest India's policy. We state that we have already lodged complaints with law and order authorities about the said fraudulent actions. Additionally, we have been made aware of certain fake applications and websites, such as https://www.iig.org.in/ and "India Investment Grid - IIG" on Google Playstore, falsely claiming to represent Invest India and/or India Investment Grid (IIG). Please be informed that these sites and applications are not endorsed by Invest India or IIG. We do not solicit money for any services, and we do not have any mobile/desktop-based applications. If you come across any other website or application claiming to represent Invest India/IIG, please report it immediately to contact@investindia.org.in, iig@investindia.org.in.
